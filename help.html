FAQs

Common questions.

How do I get a dieline?

Send the packaging type, product dimensions, material preference, and quantity. After the quote or sample request is confirmed, The Cosmo Packaging prepares the correct dieline for your selected structure.

Should I order a sample before production?

Yes. A sample helps confirm dimensions, closure, material feel, print direction, and finish quality before committing to a full production run.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most custom packaging formats start at 100 units. Complex finishes, specialty inserts, certified materials, or unusual film structures may need a higher confirmed production quantity after review.

Can The Cosmo Packaging review my artwork?

Yes. We check bleed, safe zones, CMYK setup, font outlines, barcode placement, and finishing layers before production approval.

How long does production take?

Most approved production runs take 10-20 business days depending on structure, quantity, print complexity, and finishing. Shipping time is separate.

Can I get a launch discount?

Qualified first production orders can request a launch discount when packaging is sample-approved and ordered at 100+ units.
