Technical Specs

Everything you need to know.

Corrugation

E-Flute 1.2–2mm thickness. Ideal for lightweight beauty products, subscription boxes, and sample kits.
B-Flute 2.5–3mm thickness. For heavier PR kits, multi-item shipments, and fragile glass bottles.

Materials

White (CCNB) Clay-coated news back. Best for vibrant CMYK print. At least 50% post-consumer recycled content.
Kraft / Brown Natural unbleached look. Suited for black/white print, eco-conscious brands, minimal aesthetics.

Print & Color

CMYK Offset Sharp, dotted-free print. Vibrant color reproduction across the entire box surface. Soy-based ink.
Pantone Matching Precise brand color matching. Recommended for logos and consistent brand identity across runs.

Finishes

Matte Lamination Non-reflective, soft feel. Hides fingerprints. Premium unboxing experience.
Glossy Lamination Shiny, high-impact finish. Colors pop. Best for vibrant, eye-catching designs.
Soft Touch Velvet-like matte texture. Adds a luxury tactile dimension. Popular for high-end beauty.

FAQs

Common questions.

What is the minimum order quantity? −
100 units per unique box design (size + print). Lower quantities available for sample kits.
Can I get a sample before ordering in bulk? +
Yes. Order a sample kit through our sample request page. The cost is reimbursable on your first production order.
What artwork format do you accept? +
Adobe Illustrator (AI), PDF, EPS with all text outlined. We also provide custom dieline templates after specs are confirmed. Artwork review is included in every order.
What are typical lead times? +
Sample production: 5–7 days. Bulk production: 12–18 business days after artwork approval, depending on quantity and complexity. Shipping time varies by destination.
Do you offer eco-friendly materials? +
Yes. Our standard board contains at least 50% post-consumer recycled content. Soy-based inks used for all CMYK printing. FSC-certified options available on request.
