FAQ.

common questions on quotes, samples, and production.

The practical stuff: what is needed, how the quote starts, and what happens before production.

What details are required before a quote?

Product type, size, quantity, material preference, finishing style, artwork status, delivery country, and target budget.

Can you recommend the box type?

Yes. The first step is matching the product with the right construction, material, finish, and production route.

Do you handle samples?

The workflow includes sample discussion and supplier coordination. Final sample cost and timeline depend on structure and finish.

What happens if artwork is not ready?

We route that into an artwork and dieline support path before final pricing is confirmed. See the artwork guide.

Can this work for cosmetic and skincare brands?

Yes. The positioning is built around cosmetic, skincare, ecommerce, wellness, and premium retail packaging.
