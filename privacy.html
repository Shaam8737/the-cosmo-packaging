Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The Cosmo Packaging respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through our website and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a quote request or sample kit order, we may collect:

  • Name, company/brand name, email, and phone number
  • Shipping address (sample kit orders)
  • Packaging specifications, quantities, and artwork status
  • Technical data such as browser type and submission timestamp

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to quote requests within our 24-hour quote support
  • To process free sample kit orders and coordinate shipping
  • To coordinate production, QC, and delivery of custom packaging orders
  • To improve our website and customer communication

Data Storage

Quote and order information is handled by The Cosmo Packaging on secure business systems accessible only to authorized staff and manufacturing partners fulfilling your order. A temporary copy may remain in your browser until our team confirms receipt. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Sharing With Partners

Production specifications may be shared with manufacturing partners solely to fulfill your order. Partners are bound by confidentiality and may not use your data for unrelated purposes.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing support@thecosmopackaging.com.

Cookies

This website does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Your browser may store quote or sample-kit form data locally so submissions are not lost if you refresh the page before our team responds.

Contact

Privacy inquiries: support@thecosmopackaging.com
