Tuck End Structure

Single sheet board, die-cut and creased to fold into shape. The tuck closure keeps contents secure while allowing easy access.

Industry Standard

The most widely used packaging style in beauty and cosmetics due to simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and clean presentation.

Printing & Quality

Choose the right print method.

We offer both digital and offset printing depending on quantity and quality needs.

Digital Print

Best for small runs

  • ✓ No setup or plate costs
  • ✓ Ideal for 100–1,000 units
  • ✓ Quick turnaround (5–7 days)
  • ✓ Easy artwork revisions
  • ✓ Cost-effective for short runs
Offset Print

Best for large runs

  • ✓ Lower per-unit cost at volume
  • ✓ Ideal for 1,000+ units
  • ✓ Pantone color matching
  • ✓ Superior color accuracy
  • ✓ Complex multi-color designs

Technical Specifications

Customization overview.

Everything you can customize is outlined below.

📐

Dimensions

Custom sizes based on your product. Common range: 2-12in length, 1-8in width, 0.5-6in depth.

📄

Materials

Paperboard, CCNB, Kraft, rigid board. 250-400 GSM standard. C1S or C2S. Eco-friendly options.

🖨️

Print

Digital (CMYK) 100-1000 units. Offset (CMYK+Pantone) 1000+ units. Full color, spot, or mono.

🎨

Coating

Matte lamination, gloss lamination, soft-touch, aqueous, or UV coating.

✨

Finishes

Foil stamping (gold, silver, rose gold). Embossing, debossing, spot UV, window patch.

✅

Quality

Pre-production sample approval. In-process QC. Final inspection. Color and dieline checks.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 100 units per design. Contact us for smaller quantities.

How long does production take?

10-15 working days after sample approval. Digital orders can be as fast as 5-7 days.

Can I get a sample before ordering?

Yes. Use the "Get Sample Kit" button on this page to request production samples.

Do you provide dieline templates?

Yes. Custom vector dielines are prepared after size, material, and closure are confirmed.

What file formats do you accept?

AI, PDF, EPS, CDR. CMYK, 300 DPI. Artwork assistance available if needed.

Can you match a Pantone color?

Yes. Offset printing (1000+ units) supports Pantone. Digital uses calibrated CMYK.
